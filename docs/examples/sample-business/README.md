# Sample Business — Example Configuration

This directory contains a complete brand configuration from a real business as a reference
example for how to populate the Startup OS config for your own company.

The sample business is a 24/7 AI front desk for local businesses — handling calls, texts, and web chat.

## Files

- `brand.yaml` — Brand voice, Narrative Framework, verticals, personas, and compliance config
- `verticals.yaml` — 12 vertical-specific templates with pain points, features, pricing, and language
- `personas.yaml` — 3 buyer personas with pain points, decision factors, and communication styles
- `pricing.yaml` — Pricing tiers, ROI framing, and competitive comparison
- `competitive.yaml` — Only-we statements, comparison table, and key differentiators
- `stage-messaging.yaml` — Headline and CTA templates for each awareness stage

## How to Use

Copy `brand.yaml` to `config/brand.yaml` in the root of your project and modify it
for your business. The vertical, persona, pricing, and competitive data files show the
level of detail you should aim for when filling in your own config.
